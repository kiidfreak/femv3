Welcome to the **Campaign Management Dashboard**! This guide will help you create, manage, and track challenges for our community businesses.

---

## **1. Creating a New Campaign**
Go to **Api > Campaigns** and click **"Add Campaign"**.

*   **Name**: Something catchy like *"Profile Starter Challenge"* or *"Review Hero"*.
*   **Description**: Tell the business owner what they need to do.
*   **Status**: Keep it as **Draft** until you are ready to launch.
*   **Schedule**: Set a start and end date.

> **üí° Pro Tip**: Use the **"Activate selected campaigns"** action button in the list view to launch multiple campaigns at once!

---

## **2. Adding Actions (Points & Goals)**
Inside the Campaign page, look for the **"Campaign Actions"** section. These are the tasks a business must do to earn points.

| Action Type | Recommended Points | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Add Logo** | 10 pts | The first step to a great profile. |
| **Get Verified** | 50 pts | Awarded when the church office approves them. |
| **Add 5 Products** | 30 pts | Encourages them to list their inventory. |
| **Receive First Review** | 20 pts | Helps them get social proof. |

---

## **3. Setting Up Rewards**
In the **"Rewards"** section, define what a business gets for their hard work.

*   **Badge**: Digital icon on their profile.
*   **Featured Listing**: Pulls them to the top of the homepage for `X` days.
*   **Platform Discount**: Special perks for their next renewal.

---

## **4. Tracking & Moderation**
*   **Business Progress**: View **"Business campaign progresss"** to see who is winning.
*   **Verified by Admin**: Some actions require a Church Admin to "Verify" them. Go to **"Completed actions"** to approve manual tasks.
